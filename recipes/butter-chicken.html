Herb Garlic Baked Brie


Description

Butter Chicken is one of the most popular curries at any Indian restaurant around the world. Aromatic golden chicken pieces in an incredible creamy curry sauce, this Butter Chicken recipe is one of the best you will try! You will love how easy it is to make in the comfort of your own home, especially with garlic butter rice and fresh homemade Naan bread.

Ingredients

  • Brie wheel
  • Baguettes sliced
  • 5 Garlic cloves
  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • Chili flakes
  • Thyme
  • 2 Apples

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic
  2. Checkerboard score the brie cheese wheel and set aside in a baking dish
  3. Heat up the butter and lightly sauté the garlic
  4. Preheat oven to 350°F
  5. Spread the butter garlic mixture evenly over the scored cheese
  6. Place baking dish in oven and bake for 10-15 minutes (or until nice and bubbly)
  7. While cheese is baking, toast the sliced Baguettes
  8. Once cheese is ready, season with some chili and thyme
  9. Drizzle honey on top of cheese
  10. Enjoy
