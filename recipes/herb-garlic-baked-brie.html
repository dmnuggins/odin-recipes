Herb Garlic Baked Brie


Description

Crispy shrimp are coated in a dreamy, creamy honey sauce and topped with candied walnuts. A total showstopper and so much better than takeout! Love easy dinner recipes?

Ingredients

  • Brie wheel
  • Baguettes sliced
  • 5 Garlic cloves
  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • Chili flakes
  • Thyme
  • 2 Apples

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic
  2. Checkerboard score the brie cheese wheel and set aside in a baking dish
  3. Heat up the butter and lightly sauté the garlic
  4. Preheat oven to 350°F
  5. Spread the butter garlic mixture evenly over the scored cheese
  6. Place baking dish in oven and bake for 10-15 minutes (or until nice and bubbly)
  7. While cheese is baking, toast the sliced Baguettes
  8. Once cheese is ready, season with some chili and thyme
  9. Drizzle honey on top of cheese
  10. Enjoy
